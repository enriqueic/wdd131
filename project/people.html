Oliver Cowdery

Oliver Cowdery was the main scribe that helped to transcribe the Book of Mormon and was one of the Three Witnesses.

  • Harmony, PAWhere Cowdery initially served as scribe. The priesthood was restored in the Susquehanna River nearby
  • Fayette, NYWhere he transcribed most of the Book of Mormon
  • Kirtland, OHWhere he served as Justice of the Peace and editor.

David Whitmer

One of the Three Witnesses of the Book of Mormon and the first stake president of the church.

  • Fayette, NYWhere the church was organized in the Whitmer family home
  • Independence, MOWhere he was stake president
  • Far West, MOWhere he was stake president

Edward Partridge

Edward Partridge was the first bishop of the Church. He led the Independence congregation

Newel Whitney

Newel Whitney served as a bishop in Kirtland. He opened the first Bishop's storehouse, which also served as the place of the School of the Prophets

  • Kirtland, OHWhere Newel Whitney served as bishop
