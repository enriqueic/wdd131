My homeplace

I live in the northwestern part of the North American country of Mexico, bordering the US state of California to the north. Our northern neighbors like to fondly refer to this region as 'Baja'.

It is know worldwide as an outdoor destination for its pristine deserts, arroyos, sierras, high-elevation pine forests, and swamps. On our coasts, we also have whales, dolphins, sea lions and many interesting species of fish. On the urban areas where most of the population is concentrated, the economy is dominated by export-oriented industrial manufacturing.
